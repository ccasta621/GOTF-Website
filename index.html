About Me

Hi, I'm Cristofer — a Construction Management student at BYU who loves building things, solving problems, and making work easier for the people around me. I’m drawn to projects where I can get my hands dirty, think clearly under pressure, and create systems that actually help teams stay organized and productive.

I enjoy taking messy or confusing tasks and turning them into something clean, simple, and workable. Whether it’s planning ahead, improving workflows, or figuring out how to keep a crew moving, I like finding practical solutions that make a real difference on a job.

I’m also involved in leadership development, and I’m always working on communicating more clearly, giving direction with confidence, and helping people work better together. Outside of class, you’ll usually find me sketching design ideas, exploring architecture, or learning new tools that help me grow in the construction industry.
